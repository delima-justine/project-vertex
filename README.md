# InternConnect
### A Centralized Internship and Document Management System for ROC.ph

---

## 1. System Overview
**InternConnect** is a web-based platform designed to centralize and streamline all intern-related processes at **ROC.ph**. The system encompasses the entire internship lifecycleâ€”from document submission and attendance tracking to milestone progress and coordinator communication.

The primary goal of the system is to drastically reduce administrative workload by automating manual tasks. It ensures full transparency between interns, the HR department, and school coordinators, while significantly accelerating post-internship processes such as the release of certificates and final clearances.

---

## 2. Distinct Focus & Value Proposition
> **The Solution:** A centralized digital hub integrating communication, document management, and progress tracking.

**InternConnect** specifically targets the common pain points of **miscommunication and document delays** that often occur between partner schools and ROC.ph. By moving away from fragmented communication channels (email, chat, paper), InternConnect acts as the single source of truth for all stakeholders.
