Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the Help and Support section of the Supply Management Information System (SMIS). Find answers to common questions.

The Supply Management Information System (SMIS) is a web-based platform designed to streamline supply requests, inventory management, and resource tracking across departments. It provides a centralized system for submitting, approving, and monitoring supply requests efficiently.

To submit a supply request, navigate to the Orders section and click "New Request." Fill in the required details including item description, quantity, and purpose. Submit the form and your request will be reviewed by the appropriate approver.

System requirements and configuration settings are accessible to Admin users only. Regular users can view their own requests and statuses, while Support Specialists can manage assigned tickets and requests.

You can track your requests by going to Orders > Pending/Approved/Released/Disapproved sections. Each request shows its current status, approval history, and any comments from approvers.

If you encounter a technical issue, first try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, use the Contact Support section below to reach our support team via email or phone. Include a description of the issue and any error messages you see.

Supply requests go through a multi-step approval process. Once submitted, requests are reviewed by the designated approver for your department. You will receive notifications when your request status changes. Approved requests proceed to release, while disapproved requests include comments explaining the reason.
